## Group

**Definition:** A Group is a set G together with a binary operation · on G such that the following three properties hold:

1. Associative:\
   $$a \cdot (b \cdot c) =(a \cdot b) \cdot c$$
2. Identity element: 
   $$a \cdot e = e \cdot a = a$$
3. Inverse element:
   $$a · a ^{−1} = a ^{−1}  · a = e$$

**Commutative(abelian)** group

$$a · b = b  · a$$
